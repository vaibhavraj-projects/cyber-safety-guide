About Cyber Security

Cyber security is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks.

It is important because our personal and financial information is constantly at risk online.

Glossary

  • Malware: Malicious software designed to harm your device.
  • Phishing: Fake messages trying to steal your personal info.
  • Firewall: A barrier that protects your system from unauthorized access.
  • Encryption: Encoding data so only authorized people can read it.
  • 2FA: Two-factor authentication for extra security.
  • Spam: Unwanted messages often used for scams.
  • VPN: Virtual Private Network to secure your connection.
  • Trojan: Malware disguised as legitimate software.
