Help & Reporting

If you face a cyber threat, here’s what to do:

  • Report to Police: Local cybercrime cell or cybercrime.gov.in
  • Bank Fraud: Contact your bank immediately and block accounts/cards.
  • Helplines: 155260 (National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal)
  • Email Reports: helpdesk@cybercrime.gov.in
  • Social Media Abuse: Report via platform’s official report button
