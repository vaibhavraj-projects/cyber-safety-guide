Frequently Asked Questions

What is cyber security?

Cyber security is protecting devices, networks, and data from digital attacks.

How can I report cybercrime in India?

Use the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal at cybercrime.gov.in or call 155260.

How do I make my passwords strong?

Use long, unique combinations of letters, numbers, and symbols.

What is phishing?

Phishing is when attackers trick you into giving personal info via fake emails/messages.

What is 2FA?

Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security using a code or app.
