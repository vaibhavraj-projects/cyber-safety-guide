Cyber Reality Check

Real numbers that show why cyber-safety matters — not to scare you, but to help you stay smart.

1.1 Lakh+

Daily Reports

India receives over a lakh cybercrime complaints every single day through the national portal.

₹10,000 Crore+

Financial Losses

Annual financial fraud reports show huge losses — most from simple scams like phishing and fake links.

70% Cases

Solved Faster

Cases reported within the first 24 hours are 70% more likely to be resolved quickly.

78% Teens

Face Online Risks

A large number of teenagers encounter fake accounts, scams, or cyber-bullying at least once.

90% Scams

Begin With a Link

Almost all cyber fraud starts with a suspicious message, email, or link — usually avoidable.

6 out of 10

Cases Preventable

Most cyber-incidents can be prevented with basic precautions like strong passwords and reporting early.

200 Million+

Indian Users Protected

Government and cybersecurity agencies block millions of harmful websites and attacks every year.

Top 3

Most Targeted

India is among the top 3 most targeted countries globally — simply because we have so many internet users.
