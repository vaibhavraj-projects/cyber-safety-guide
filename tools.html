Preventative Tools

  • Strong Passwords: Use long, unique passwords.
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Adds extra security layer.
  • Anti-virus: Keep devices protected from malware.
  • Spam Blockers: Avoid phishing and malicious emails.
  • Safe Browsing: Use secure browsers, VPNs, and HTTPS sites.
