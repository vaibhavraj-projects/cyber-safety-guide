Essential Cyber Safety Tools

Staying safe online isn’t just about what you know — it’s also about what you use. These simple tools help protect your accounts, devices, and personal information while you explore the internet with confidence.

Your Digital Toolkit for Safer Browsing

Antivirus Software

Protects your device from viruses, malware, and harmful files.

Examples: Windows Defender, Avast, Quick Heal

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Adds a second verification step—like an OTP—to keep accounts secure.

Examples: Google Authenticator, Authy, SMS OTP

Verified Browsing Apps

Warns you about unsafe websites and blocks risky downloads.

Examples: Chrome Safe Browsing, Firefox, Edge

Strong Password Generator

Creates long, unique passwords that are almost impossible to guess.

Examples: Chrome Generator, LastPass, 1Password

Spam & Scam Blockers

Filters fake emails, scam messages, and suspicious links.

Examples: Gmail Filter, Truecaller, Outlook Junk Filter

Safe Payment Tools

Keeps your online transactions secure and protected.

Examples: UPI with PIN, Razorpay, Paytm
