Common Online Threats

  • Phishing: Fake emails or messages trying to steal information. Tip: Never click unknown links!
  • Malware: Software that harms your device. Tip: Keep antivirus updated!
  • Ransomware: Locks your files and asks for money. Tip: Backup your files regularly.
  • Identity Theft: Stealing personal information. Tip: Don’t share passwords.
  • Online Scams: Fake offers to trick you. Tip: Verify sources before buying.
  • Social Media Hacking: Accessing your accounts illegally. Tip: Enable 2FA.
  • Spyware: Tracks your online activity. Tip: Avoid unknown downloads.
  • Cyberbullying: Harassment online. Tip: Report and block offenders.
