Common Online Threats

Understand the risks you face online—and how to avoid them.

1. Phishing

Phishing is when attackers try to trick you into giving personal information by pretending to be someone trustworthy—like a bank, school, or delivery service.

ℹNever click links from unknown or suspicious emails.

2. Malware

Malware is harmful software that can steal data, lock your device, or spy on your online activity. It often hides in fake downloads or shady websites.

ℹDownload apps only from official stores and trusted websites.

3. Identity Theft

Identity theft happens when someone steals your personal information and uses it to impersonate you online or access your accounts.

ℹUse strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

4. Social Engineering

Social engineering tricks people into revealing private information through manipulation, emotional pressure, or fake emergencies.

ℹPause and verify before sharing ANY personal details.

5. Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying includes threatening, insulting, or harassing someone online. It affects mental health and can escalate quickly.

ℹBlock, mute, and report harmful users immediately.

6. Fake Apps & Websites

Some websites or apps pretend to be real but actually steal your data or money.

ℹCheck the URL and reviews before downloading or logging in.

7. Online Scams

Scams include fake prizes, job offers, giveaways, or deals that seem too good to be true.

ℹIf it sounds too good to be true—it is.

8. Ransomware

Ransomware locks your device and demands money for access. It spreads through infected files or unsafe links.

ℹBackup your files and avoid downloading unknown attachments.

9. Data Breaches

Data breaches occur when hackers break into websites and steal user information like passwords and emails.

ℹUse different passwords for every account.

10. Catfishing

Catfishing is when someone pretends to be someone else online to manipulate or exploit others.

ℹNever trust strangers without verifying their identity.

"Stay smart. Stay safe. Stay cyber secure."
